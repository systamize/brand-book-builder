Brand Foundation

Before colors and fonts, there's a reason. Verdant exists to make home goods worth keeping — things built with care, from materials you can trace, that get better with time.

Verdant makes home goods built to last — textiles, storage, and surfaces sourced and crafted with full transparency. Started with a single line of European linen in 2019, the brand has grown into a complete home collection grounded in one belief: the most sustainable thing you can buy is something you never have to replace.

Brand Values

⌛

Longevity over trend

We make things that outlast seasons. If it won't look right in ten years, we don't make it.

🌿

Material honesty

We tell you what's in every product and where it came from. We also flag what we don't know yet.

🤫

Quiet confidence

Good things don't need loud marketing. We let the quality speak. No exaggeration, no urgency.

▫

Less, better

A smaller range of things made right. Restraint is a design decision.

Brand Personality

GroundedHonestWarm DeliberateUnderstatedConsidered NaturalPreciseUnhurriedTrustworthy

Who we're talking to

Homeowners and renters, 30–50, who are done replacing cheap things every two years. They research before buying, care about supply chains without being preachy about it, and appreciate products that come with a specific story — not a vague sustainability claim.

05

Imagery & Visual Style

Real homes, real light, real texture. Not aspirational staging.

Photography Direction

  • Natural daylight — window light preferred
  • Real homes and lived-in spaces
  • Material texture front and center
  • Warm, slightly muted color grading

What to Avoid

  • Perfectly styled interiors that look unliveable
  • High-contrast editing or trendy filters
  • Generic stock imagery
  • Cold, blue-tinted tones

Illustration & Graphics

  • Minimal and functional only
  • Single-weight line art, brand colors only
  • No decorative illustration

Iconography

  • Line style, 1.5px stroke, rounded ends
  • 24×24px base grid
  • Lucide or Phosphor (Regular weight)
06

Voice & Tone

Always grounded, always specific. The tone shifts to fit context, but we never sound rushed, never corporate.

Grounded

In practice: material-first, plain language

Not "sustainably crafted" — "370gsm waffle linen, certified European flax, made in Portugal." The detail is the point.

Honest

In practice: say what you don't know too

Trust is built by being specific about limits, not just strengths.

Warm

In practice: personal, not corporate

A knowledgeable friend — direct, but never cold. Never a press release.

Unhurried

In practice: short sentences, no urgency tactics

We write like we make things: without rushing. No manufactured scarcity.

✕ Off-brand

"Our eco-conscious, sustainably-sourced, planet-positive products help you live your best sustainable life while reducing your carbon footprint and elevating your living space!"

✓ On-brand

"Linen washed three times for softness. Certified European flax. No synthetic blends, no shortcuts."
