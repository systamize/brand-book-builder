# Verdant — Brand Reference

## Brand Foundation

- **Mission:** Make home goods worth keeping — built with care, from materials you can trace, that get better with time.
- **Overview:** Verdant makes home goods built to last — textiles, storage, and surfaces sourced and crafted with full transparency. Started with a single line of European linen in 2019, grown into a complete home collection grounded in one belief: the most sustainable thing you can buy is something you never have to replace.
- **Target audience:** Homeowners and renters, 30–50, who are done replacing cheap things every two years. They research before buying, care about supply chains without being preachy about it, and appreciate products that come with a specific story — not a vague sustainability claim. They buy less, but they buy well.

### Brand Values

1. **Longevity over trend** — Things that outlast seasons. If it won't look right in ten years, we don't make it.
2. **Material honesty** — Tell you what's in every product and where it came from. Also flag what we don't know yet. No vague sustainability claims.
3. **Quiet confidence** — Good things don't need loud marketing. We let quality speak. No exaggeration, no urgency, no greenwashing.
4. **Less, better** — A smaller range of things made right. Restraint is a design decision.

### Brand Personality

Grounded · Honest · Warm · Deliberate · Understated · Considered · Natural · Precise · Unhurried · Trustworthy

> "The friend with a beautifully simple home who always knows the name of the maker. Knowledgeable without being preachy. Warm without being effusive."

## Logo

- **Mark description:** A single leaf — simple, organic, readable at any size. No gradients, no effects. References growth and natural materials without being literal.
- **Wordmark:** Leaf mark + "Verdant" set in Playfair Display 700

### Variants

| Variant | Fill | Background |
|---|---|---|
| Primary — Icon on Forest | White (with Forest veins) | #2D4A3E |
| Icon on White | Forest #2D4A3E (with white veins) | #FFFFFF |
| Icon on Charcoal | White (with Charcoal veins) | #1C1C1A |
| Icon on Terracotta | Ivory #F5F0E8 (with Terracotta veins) | #C4613A |

- **Clearspace:** Minimum 1× the mark height on all sides.
- **Approved backgrounds:** Forest (#2D4A3E), White (#FFFFFF), Charcoal (#1C1C1A), Terracotta (#C4613A)

### Never

- Stretch or distort the mark
- Use at low opacity
- Place on Sage or other non-approved backgrounds
- Add drop shadows, glows, or outlines

## Typography

- **Heading typeface:** Playfair Display — Bold 700 and ExtraBold 800
- **Body typeface:** DM Sans — variable, weights 300–700
- **Fallback stack:** -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif

### Typographic Scale

| Level | Typeface | Weight | Letter Spacing | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| H1 | Playfair Display | 700 | −3% | Hero, page titles |
| H2 | Playfair Display | 700 | −2% | Section headings |
| H3 | Playfair Display | 700 | −1% | Sub-headings |
| H4 | DM Sans | 600 | 0 | Card titles, functional headings |
| Body | DM Sans | 400 | 0 | 1rem / 1.7 line-height |
| Caption | DM Sans | 400 | 0 | 0.82rem |
| Label | DM Sans | 700 | +12% | Uppercase, small caps style |

### Rules

- Use negative letter spacing on all Playfair headings (−1% to −3%)
- Set body text at minimum 16px / 1.7 line-height
- Reserve Playfair Display for H3 and above only
- Left-align body text
- Never use Playfair for body copy or UI labels
- Never set body text below 14px

## Imagery & Visual Style

### Photography

- Natural daylight — window light preferred, no studio flash
- Real homes and lived-in spaces, not showrooms
- Material texture front and center: weave, grain, surface detail
- Warm, slightly muted color grading — earthy, not saturated

### Avoid

- Perfectly styled interiors that look unliveable
- High-contrast editing or trendy filters
- Generic stock imagery
- Cold, blue-tinted tones

### Iconography

- Style: line, 1.5px stroke, rounded end caps
- Base grid: 24×24px
- Recommended sets: Lucide or Phosphor (Regular weight)

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice

- **Grounded:** Material-first, plain language. Not "sustainably crafted" — "370gsm waffle linen, certified European flax, made in Portugal."
- **Honest:** Say what you don't know as well as what you do. Trust is built by being specific about limits, not just strengths.
- **Warm:** Personal, not corporate. A knowledgeable friend, not a FAQ.
- **Unhurried:** Short sentences. No urgency tactics. No manufactured scarcity.

### Tone by Context

- Product descriptions: material-first, specific, tactile. No adjectives we can't defend.
- Social media: quiet and observational. Share a specific detail, not a campaign.
- Email: personal, as if from the founder. Short paragraphs, no urgency.
- Error messages: clear and calm. Acknowledge, then resolve. No drama.

### Vocabulary

**Use:** materials, made, origin, craft, linen, natural, last, considered, honest, specific

**Avoid:** eco-friendly, sustainable journey, planet-positive, curated, artisanal, elevate, luxurious, timeless

### Key Messages

- **Tagline:** "Made to last. Made to matter."
- **Elevator pitch:** "Verdant makes home goods built to outlast trends — textiles, storage, and surfaces sourced and crafted with full transparency."

## On-Brand vs Off-Brand Writing

- OFF: "Our eco-conscious, sustainably-sourced, planet-positive products help you live your best sustainable life while reducing your carbon footprint and elevating your living space!"
- ON: "Linen washed three times for softness. Certified European flax. No synthetic blends, no shortcuts."

- OFF: "⚡ FLASH SALE — 48 HOURS ONLY! Don't miss out! LIMITED STOCK — add to cart NOW before it's gone forever!!!"
- ON: "We're clearing last season's stock at reduced prices. Same quality, same materials. When it's gone, it's gone."
